Some Famous Anime

  • NARUT0

    “Naruto” is a Japanese manga series by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The story is divided into two parts: Naruto’s pre-teen years and his teenage years. The manga was adapted into an anime television series.

  • ATTACK_ON_TITEN

    “Attack on Titan” is a manga series by Hajime Isayama. It’s set in a world where humans live in cities protected by walls from Titans, giant man-eating humanoids. The story follows Eren Yeager, who vows to exterminate the Titans after they destroy his hometown and kill his mother. The manga was adapted into an anime series.

  • DRAGON_BALL- Z

    “Dragon Ball Z” is an anime series that continues the adventures of Goku from the original “Dragon Ball” series. It follows Goku and his companions as they defend Earth against various villains. The series also explores the life of Goku’s son, Gohan, and other characters from the “Dragon Ball” series. It was followed by sequel series “Dragon Ball GT” and “Dragon Ball Super”.

  • JUJUTSU_KAISEN

    “Jujutsu Kaisen” is a manga series by Gege Akutami. It’s about Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes the host of a powerful Curse named Ryomen Sukuna. The manga has been serialized since March 2018 and was adapted into an anime series. The manga was adapted into an anime television series, which aired from October 2020 to December 2023.
